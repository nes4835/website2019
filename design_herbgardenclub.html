The Herb Garden Club Book Design & Illustration

I was approached by author Kathy Stevens to visually design and illustrate her book, The Herb Garden Club. Intended for a middle grade audience, the mix of information, narrative, and recipes proved an exciting challenge to illustrate and lay out. The book took on a new life with over 70 spot illustrations, 7 full page paintings, and borders customized to each chapter and section.

Timeless Herbal Traditions Website Design

After working with author Kathy Stevens on her book "The Herb Garden Club," I designed, illustrated, and developed her website "Timeless Herbal Traditions." Intended for a wider audience than her book, we developed an "Apothocary-esque" aesthetic designed to invoke a timeless feel.
